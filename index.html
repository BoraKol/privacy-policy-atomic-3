Atomic 3

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Atomic 3 ("we", "us", or "the App") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

Atomic 3 is designed to help you track tasks and improve your focus. To provide cross-device synchronization and a seamless experience, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address via Firebase Authentication.
  • Task Data: We store the task descriptions and completion statuses you enter within the App.
  • Usage Statistics: We store metrics such as total completed tasks, focus session durations, and activity logs to provide you with personal progress summaries.

2. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Firebase: Used for authentication, cloud database storage, and notifications.
  • Expo: Used for application framework and distribution.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure. We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. User Rights and Data Deletion

We respect your privacy and provide you with full control over your data:

  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time through the App settings. When you delete your account, all your personal data and task history will be permanently and irrevocably removed from our servers.

5. Advertising ID (AD_ID)

The App may access your device's Android Advertising ID for analytics and core app functionality purposes. This identifier is used to understand user engagement and improve the App's performance. You can reset or opt-out of this tracking at any time through your device's Android settings.

6. Age Limit

Our application is intended for individuals aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under the age of 13.
